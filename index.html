I'm a researcher, student, and speaker from Waterloo, Canada. Currently a Health Sciences student @ Queen's University as a Chancellor's Scholar.

Recently, I am —

  • → Conducting orthopaedic research @ UAB School of Medicine & Waterloo Health Network. Fascinated by knee osteoarthritis and clinical research on musculoskeletal function.
  • → Building RAG models with a Google engineer to make emerging companies more sustainable.
  • → Fascinated by global health. In high school, I conducted sociology & global health projects @ Harvard, WHO, Square Ltd., and the New York Academy of Sciences. Currently working with Global Medical Brigades to fundraise & volunteer for underserved communities abroad (i.e. Ghana!).
  • → I love speaking & connecting with others (s/o Socratica!). Spoken/presented @ TEDx, CASEM, HOSA, CUCOH & local high schools + community events/podcasts.

I love reading, stand-up comedy, travelling, soccer, and chess. Occasionally I write, and I thrive on saying yes to last-minute, spontaneous adventures.
